# WB Objection Trainer — How to Use

Your personal sales practice partner. Run real practice calls, get coached like a master rep, and sharpen your objection handling — anytime, on any device.

**Your app:** https://wb-objection-trainer.vercel.app

> 💡 **Use Chrome or Edge on a computer, or Chrome on Android, for full voice.** On iPhone/iPad, voice (talking out loud) doesn't work — you can still train by typing your replies.

---

## Getting started (60 seconds)

1. Open the link above.
2. **Sign up** with your email and a password (first time), or **Sign in** if you already have an account.
3. You'll land on the home screen. That's it — start practicing.

## The home screen — set up your call

Before you pick a mode, set two things at the top:

**1. Difficulty** — how tough the prospect is:
| Level | Who you're facing |
|---|---|
| **1 — Believer** | Warm, open, easy. Good for warming up. |
| **2 — Trainer** | Realistic everyday prospect. |
| **3 — Builder** | Guarded, makes you work for it. |
| **4 — Leader** | Brutal, hostile, stacked objections. Near-elite required. |

**2. How the call was booked** — sets the prospect's opening attitude (referral, DM, met in person, thought it was a job, etc.). This changes how warm or skeptical they start.

## The three ways to train

### 🎭 THE PROSPECT — *build your conversation muscle*
A full practice call. A realistic prospect talks, you talk back. Your job: warm them up, run discovery, uncover their real **WHY**, handle objections, and bridge to the close.
- Watch the **WHY meter** climb as you get deeper — it only rises when you genuinely earn it.
- Type your replies, or tap **📞** for a hands-free live voice call.

### 🧑‍🏫 LEARN FROM RAJA — *watch the master*
The script flips: **Raja** (a master rep) runs the call and **you play the client**. Feel what elite discovery, getting the WHY, and smooth objection handling sound like from the receiving end.
- After the call, hit **End & Recap** to see exactly what Raja did and copy his best lines.
- Then **Switch roles** to run the same client yourself.

### 💥 THE GAUNTLET — *pattern recognition*
Rapid-fire drills. An objection drops, you respond out loud (or typed), and you get **scored** against your frameworks. Great for reps.

---

## During a call

- **📞 Live call:** tap it to go hands-free — talk, and the prospect talks back. Tap the speaking indicator to interrupt.
- **💡 Hint:** stuck? Tap it for coaching. It tells you the exact move to make right now — and *when* to use the **Pullback**, **Bridge**, **Pain Bridge**, or **Must Conversion**. Nothing pops up on its own; you ask when you want it.
  - **Pullback** → the prospect is pushing back or feeling pressure — release it.
  - **Pain Bridge** → they're committed but stalling — make them feel the cost of staying put.
  - **Must Conversion** → they're interested but not committed.
  - **Bridge (New Art of Living)** → only once the WHY is real and felt.
- **End & Debrief:** finish the call to get scored feedback on your discovery, WHY, tonality, and close.

## The other tools

### 🛟 Redempter — *fix a real call*
Paste a real objection you got stuck on, or upload an entire Zoom/call transcript. Raja shows you how he'd redeem it. Find it on the home screen.

### 📊 History & Patterns
See your past sessions, scores, and how you're trending over time.

### ⚙️ Settings (during a call)
- **Voice calibration:** read one line out loud once, and the AI matches its speaking pace and pitch to feel natural to you.
- **Pick a voice** and adjust the silence window (how long it waits before you've "finished talking").

---

## Voice tips

- **Allow microphone access** when the browser asks — voice won't work without it.
- Use **headphones** if you can — it stops the AI from hearing itself through your speakers and interrupting.
- The AI **listens to your tone** — talk with energy and it matches you; talk soft and guarded and it softens too.
- On a phone, keep the screen on during a live call (a screen lock can cut the mic).

---

## Quick troubleshooting

| Problem | Fix |
|---|---|
| Voice won't work | Use Chrome/Edge on a computer or Chrome on Android. iPhone doesn't support it — type instead. |
| Mic not picking up | Allow microphone in your browser (tap the address-bar lock/⋮ → Site settings → Microphone → Allow). |
| "Connection error" | The AI is briefly busy — wait a few seconds and try again. |
| It interrupts me | Use headphones, or lengthen the silence window in ⚙️ Settings. |
